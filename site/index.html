Why this is an agent, not a server

Your node sets its own price — it surges when busy, discounts when idle — and advertises itself so other agents can discover and hire it. The buyers are agents too: they find your node, decide if your price fits their budget, pay per second, and stop when they have enough. Software paying software, with USDC on Arc. Your laptop becomes an economic agent in a machine-to-machine market.
